This project follows the [all-contributors](https://github.com/all-contributors/all-contributors) specification. Contributions of **any** kind are welcome to be added to [this list](https://github.com/thecloudsucks/thecloudsucks.guide/blob/main/.all-contributorsrc), including contributions to The Cloud Sucks outside this repo, and contributions that aren't content related (like sharing ideas for The Cloud Sucks, promoting the project, answering questions on the forum, etc.).

| Emoji | Type | Description
| --- | --- | ---
| 📖 | `doc` | A contributor to the content on [thecloudsucks.guide](https://www.thecloudsucks.guide/en).
| 👀 | `review` | Someone who has taken the time to review [pull requests](https://github.com/thecloudsucks/thecloudsucks.guide/pulls) to the site.
| 📝 | `blog` | Someone who has written a [blog](https://blog.thecloudsucks.guide) post for us.
| 💬 | `question` | Someone who has been helpful when answering questions on our [forum](https://discuss.thecloudsucks.guide) or Matrix channels.
| 🌍 | `translation` | Someone who has contributed on [Crowdin](https://crowdin.com/project/privacyguides).

A huge thank you from The Cloud Sucks to the following wonderful people ([full emoji key](https://allcontributors.org/docs/en/emoji-key)). We also especially thank our dedicated community moderation team on Matrix and our forum: *Austin Huang*, *namazso*, *hik*, *riley*, and *Valynor*.
